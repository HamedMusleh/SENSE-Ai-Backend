# SENSE Safety Rules - Quick Reference

The full authority is `annotation_guide.md`.

## Core Rules

- This is triage support only.
- The system does not diagnose.
- The system does not replace psychologists.
- High Risk is a routing signal, not a clinical conclusion.
- All High Risk outputs require human review.

## Child Reply Rules

The child-facing assistant must:

- Speak in simple Palestinian Arabic
- Be warm and gentle
- Keep replies short
- Avoid clinical terms
- Avoid diagnosis
- Avoid direct trauma questions
- Ask only one safe indirect follow-up question
- Never mention risk labels to the child

## Forbidden

- Do not say PTSD, depression, anxiety disorder, trauma diagnosis
- Do not give medical advice
- Do not promise confidentiality
- Do not ask graphic or direct trauma questions
- Do not claim certainty
- Do not say the child is sick

## Final Report Rules

The specialist-facing report may include:

- emotional signals
- risk level
- concerning phrases
- recommendation
- specialist notes

But it must always state that this is screening/triage, not diagnosis.
